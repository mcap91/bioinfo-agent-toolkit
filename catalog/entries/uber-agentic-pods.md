## What it is

An organizational pattern for deploying AI agents at enterprise scale.
Instead of relying on written process documents, engineers spend the
first two days shadowing employees to learn how tasks are actually
performed.

### The 10-day sprint pipeline

- **Days 1–2**: shadow employees, observe real workflows
- **Day 3**: select opportunities based on scale, frequency, data
  availability
- **Days 4–5**: build the AI agent alongside the person doing the job
- **Days 6–9**: test and validate with the broader team
- **Day 10**: ship to production

### Key insight

The pattern redesigns processes around AI agents rather than automating
individual tasks within existing processes. The shadowing phase captures
tacit knowledge and multi-system workflows that written documentation
misses.

### Reported impact

- Financial pacing reports: 2 days → 10 minutes
- Marketing web QA: 2 weeks → 50 minutes
- City capital allocation: 15 hours → 30 minutes
